About Dan

Hi there! My name is Dan M and I'm a frontend development trainer with over 15 years of experience in the industry. I'm passionate about teaching people the skills they need to create stunning and user-friendly web experiences.

When I'm not coding or teaching, you can usually find me browsing the latest memes on Reddit. In fact, I like to joke that memes are the frontend of the internet - they're the first thing that people see and they have the power to make or break a website's popularity. So if you're looking to spice up your frontend development skills with some meme-worthy techniques, I'm your guy!

But in all seriousness, I believe that a strong foundation in frontend development is essential for anyone looking to succeed in the digital world. From HTML and CSS to JavaScript and beyond, I'm here to help you master the tools and techniques that will make your websites stand out from the crowd.

So if you're ready to take your frontend development skills to the next level, let's get started! And who knows, maybe we'll even create a meme or two along the way ;)
